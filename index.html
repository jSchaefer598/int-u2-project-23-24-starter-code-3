Trash Talk: How Garbage is Destroying the Environment

Plants

The amount of trash on the ground is keeping the plants that are necessary for the environment from growing. The trash is covering the space that these essential plants need in order to grow. We need trees to filter carbon dioxide and turn it back into fresh air.

Solution

If we as people come together to pick up some of the trash around us, then we would have a much cleaner environment. With a cleaner environment, the things that keep us alive will survive and we will thrive.
